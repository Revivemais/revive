The Science

A- Beetroot extract:

Beetroot is an antioxidant and has anti-inflammatory properties.
It is also a great source of nitrate, which is converted in the body to a gas called nitric oxide.
Beetroot is also rich in phytochemicals, protecting the heart and blood vessels and improving blood flow.
Beetroot has been shown to help to reduce the blood pressure and the delivery of oxygen to the muscles.
It has a nutritional importance in cognitive and physical functions, and helps fight against a range of diseases such as cardiovascular diseases and conditions (including hypertension), dementia, cancer and diabetes.

B- Glucosamine and chondroitin:

This product has a great combination of nutrients that have are known for their beneficial impact on health.
Glucosamine is naturally found in the body and aids cartilage formation. As a food supplement, it is considered to be beneficial for the joints, especially in osteoarthritis.
Our product also contains vitamin C; a vitamin with antioxidant properties which helps maintain healthy bone and cartilage cells. Vitamin C also contributes to normal collagen formation aiding the maintenance of bones, cartilage and skin.
In addition, rosehip extract has been shown to help lose weight and reduce blood sugar in animals.
